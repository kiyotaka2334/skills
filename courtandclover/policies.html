Policies

Plain-language store policies. Have a lawyer look these over before treating them as more than a good-faith promise.

Privacy policy

We collect only what we need to run the store: your email if you join the list, and your name, shipping address, and order details when you buy. Payment information is handled entirely by our payment processors — we never see or store card numbers.

Order details are shared with our print and fulfillment partner solely to produce and ship your order. We use standard analytics to understand how the site is used. We do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

Want your data deleted, or a copy of what we hold? Email hello@courtandclover.com and we'll handle it within 30 days.

Terms of service

By ordering from Court & Clover you agree to these basics: all products are made to order and produced after payment; prices are in US dollars and exclude any applicable taxes shown at checkout; the returns policy on our shipping & returns page is the returns policy referenced here and applies to all orders.

All designs, copy, and imagery on this site belong to Court & Clover and may not be reproduced commercially without permission. We may update these terms; the version published at the time of your order applies to it.

Refund policy

Refunds and replacements follow the returns policy stated on the shipping & returns page: free replacement for defects, misprints, and carrier errors; no returns for sizing choices on made-to-order garments. Approved refunds go back to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

About the fit: the 1717 is a boxy, relaxed cut — that's the garment's character, not a sizing mistake. Between sizes? The fit is relaxed — size down for a closer fit. The fastest way to get it right: measure a tee they already wear and match the width to the chart.

Shipping

Every piece is printed to order — nothing sits in a warehouse. That means two clocks run on your order:

  • Production: 2–5 business days to print your shirt.
  • Transit: 3–4 business days for US delivery.

Most US orders arrive 5–9 business days after checkout. Shipping is free within the US — it's already in the price, no code needed.

Ordering for an occasion? Order at least 10 days before the date and you'll be comfortably ahead of both clocks.

Returns

Plain language, no fine print:

  • Defects and misprints: we replace them free. Email a photo to hello@courtandclover.com within 30 days of delivery — no need to ship anything back.
  • Sizing: because every shirt is printed just for you, we can't accept returns for size choices. That's why the exact measurement chart above is on every product page — please check it before ordering.
  • Wrong or lost package: if the carrier loses it or delivers the wrong item, we make it right at no cost.

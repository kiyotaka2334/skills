Our story

The game deserved better shirts

Walk through any pickleball gift search and you'll find the same thing: neon clipart, a pun in a default font, printed on the thinnest blank the seller could source. The sport that filled municipal courts and country clubs alike — the one your father, your neighbor, and your doctor all play — gets dressed like a joke.

Court & Clover exists because we didn't think the joke had to look like one. A funny shirt can still be a well-made shirt. So every design here is printed on the Comfort Colors 1717 — a heavyweight, garment-dyed cotton tee that's been the quiet favorite of people who care about blanks for decades. It costs more than the usual print-on-demand canvas. It's worth it the first time you pull it on.

Everything is printed to order, in the USA. Not because it's faster — it isn't — but because it means nothing sits in a warehouse waiting to be marked down, and every piece that ships was made for the person it's going to. When your order comes in, a real printer in a real facility pulls a fresh blank and makes your shirt. That takes a few days. We think the trade is honest, and we say so on every page rather than burying it.

We're a small independent brand — genuinely small, the kind where the person writing this also answers the email. If something isn't right with your order, tell us and we'll fix it. If it's perfect, tell the friend who keeps losing to you at the kitchen line.

FAQ

How long until it arrives?

Each piece is printed for you in 2–5 business days, then ships. Most US orders arrive within 5–9 business days of ordering.

How does the sizing run?

Relaxed and boxy. Between sizes? Size down for a closer fit. Exact measurements are on every product page.

What if something's wrong?

Defects and misprints are replaced free — just email a photo within 30 days.
